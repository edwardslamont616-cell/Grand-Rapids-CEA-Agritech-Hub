## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**1. Market Risks**
- **Risk:** Competition from traditional agriculture
- **Mitigation:** Focus on premium quality, year-round availability, local story

**2. Technology Risks**
- **Risk:** System failures or performance below expectations
- **Mitigation:** Proven technology partners, redundant systems, phased rollout

**3. Operational Risks**
- **Risk:** Inability to achieve target yields or quality
- **Mitigation:** Experienced team, pilot testing, continuous optimization

**4. Financial Risks**
- **Risk:** Cost overruns, revenue shortfalls
- **Mitigation:** Conservative projections, phased investment, multiple revenue streams

**5. Energy Cost Risks**
- **Risk:** Electricity price volatility
- **Mitigation:** Renewable energy integration, efficient systems, hedging strategies

### Mitigation Strategies

- **Diversified Revenue:** Multiple products and services
- **Modular Approach:** Scale operations based on demand
- **Strong Partnerships:** Technology, academic, community
- **Experienced Team:** Track record in agriculture, technology, real estate
- **Conservative Underwriting:** Buffer in projections
