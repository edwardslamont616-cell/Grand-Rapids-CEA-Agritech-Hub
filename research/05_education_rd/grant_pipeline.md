# Grant Pipeline — Grand Rapids CEA Agritech Hub
## Education, R&D, and Workforce Programming
**Prepared by:** Prof. Elena Cruz, CEA Campus Design Council
**Frameworks:** Five S + TRACE + Co-Star
**Date:** 2026 | *Note: All deadlines should be independently verified at source URLs before application*

---

## Overview

The Hub's grant strategy is organized in three tiers:
- **Tier 1 (Priority — Apply Y1):** High fit, near-term deadlines, manageable application burden
- **Tier 2 (Pipeline — Apply Y2):** Strong fit but require pilot data or institutional partnerships not yet in place
- **Tier 3 (Horizon — Apply Y3+):** Large competitive grants requiring track record; begin relationship-building now

Total realistic Y1 grant revenue: **$300,000–$500,000** (primarily from Tier 1 sources)
Total realistic Y2–Y3 pipeline: **$1.5M–$5M** (if Y1 pilots produce publishable data)
