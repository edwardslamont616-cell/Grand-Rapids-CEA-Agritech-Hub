## Purpose

This folder is the single source of truth for due-diligence-grade evidence behind every claim in the Grand Rapids CEA Agritech Hub investor decks, grant applications, and permit submissions. Each domain folder contains research memos, datasets, benchmark tables, and drafted outbound communications. `SOURCES.md` is the master bibliography — every numeric or regulatory claim must cite a row.

## Research loop (applies to every domain)

1. **Scope** — one-sentence research brief per question
2. **Source hierarchy** — primary (law/code/data/interviews) → secondary (peer-reviewed, extension) → tertiary (trade press)
3. **Collect** — instruments: FOIA, expert interviews, site visits, datasets, literature, standards
4. **Triangulate** — every claim ≥ 3 independent sources before it is quotable
5. **Version & cite** — `SOURCES.md` + access-dates + quote-logs

## Citation convention

Inline: `[Source Title](URL) — accessed YYYY-MM-DD`
In `SOURCES.md`: numbered entries with full bibliographic data.
