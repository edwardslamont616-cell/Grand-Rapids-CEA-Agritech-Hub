# Marketing Launch Plan — 6-Month Pre-Open
**Grand Rapids CEA Agritech Hub**
*Prepared by Jordan Rivers, Hospitality & Agri-Tourism Specialist | CEA Campus Design Council*
*Framework: RACE (Reach, Act, Convert, Engage)*

---

## Launch Calendar Overview

| Phase | Timing | RACE Stage | Primary Goal |
|-------|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Foundation | T–6 months | Reach | Brand presence established; stakeholder buy-in secured |
| Buzz Build | T–4 months | Reach + Act | Email list 2,000+; social following 1,000+ |
| Pre-Sale | T–10 weeks | Act + Convert | Founding Memberships + pre-sold tour tickets; $35K pre-revenue target |
| Final Push | T–4 weeks | Convert + Engage | FAM tours; media coverage; capacity pre-sold |
| Launch Week | T–0 | Engage | Grand opening event; earned media surge; member community live |
| Post-Open | +4 to +12 weeks | Engage | Retention, reviews, referral activation |

---

## Month 1 (T–6): Foundation

**Goal:** Brand infrastructure live; partnership conversations initiated; team aligned

### Deliverables
- [ ] Brand identity finalized (name, wordmark, tagline, color palette — recommend: deep green + honey gold + Grand River slate blue)
- [ ] Website launched: homepage, about, experiences, booking/waitlist CTA, news/blog
- [ ] Social profiles created: Instagram (primary), Facebook (events/community), LinkedIn (B2B corporate retreats + education)
- [ ] Email list seeding: founders' network, GVSU, Davenport, GRPS contacts, hospitality industry contacts
- [ ] Partner outreach initiated (EXGR, DGRI, GR Whitewater — formal letters; see outbound_drafts/)
- [ ] Downtown Market GR + Hotel District initial outreach

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Brand identity (agency or freelance) | $4,000–8,000 |
| Website design + development | $5,000–12,000 |
| Initial photography/video shoot (construction progress) | $2,000–4,000 |
| Domain, hosting, email platform (Mailchimp/Klaviyo) | $500/year |
| **Month 1 Total** | **$11,500–24,500** |

---

## Month 2 (T–5): Story Building

**Goal:** Content library established; media relationships warmed; early community forming

### Deliverables
- [ ] "Building the Hub" content series launches: bi-weekly behind-the-scenes Instagram/Facebook posts showing construction, crop systems going live, hive installation
- [ ] Blog/newsletter launched: monthly "From the Growing Floor" email (target: 500 subscribers by month end)
- [ ] First media pitch sent to MLive, Rapid Growth Media, Grand Rapids Magazine: "Grand Rapids is about to grow its own food downtown"
- [ ] Founding Member waitlist opens: $95–135 household membership, founding-tier benefits (first-ever harvest share, permanent 20% workshop discount, name recognition at facility)
- [ ] EXGR attraction partner application submitted

### Content Themes (RACE "Act" — inspire engagement)
1. **"Meet the Growers"** — Introduce Dr. Maya Green, Amira Bloom, other council members as real people with expertise
2. **"The River Comes Alive"** — Tie Hub narrative to GRWW restoration; "the river is coming back, and so is the food it makes possible"
3. **"Grand Rapids Grows"** — Food system identity content; where does GR's food actually come from today?

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Monthly content creation (photography + video) | $1,500–3,000/month |
| Email marketing platform (Klaviyo or Mailchimp) | $150–300/month |
| PR outreach (in-house or publicist) | $1,000–2,500/month |
| Social ad spend (targeted: GR residents, food/nature interest) | $500–1,000/month |
| **Month 2 Total** | **$3,150–6,800** |

## Month 3 (T–4): Buzz Build

**Goal:** 2,000+ email subscribers; 1,500+ social followers; first earned media placements

### Deliverables
- [ ] First major media feature: target Rapid Growth Media or MLive long-form story
- [ ] "Countdown to Opening" Instagram series launches (60-day countdown)
- [ ] Pop-up presence at Downtown Market GR (Sat. outdoor market): Hub-grown samples + membership enrollment, no charge — pure list-building and brand awareness
- [ ] First paid social campaign: "Be a Founding Member" targeting Kent County adults 25–55, interest in food/sustainability/local
- [ ] Group sales deck completed for corporate retreat + school field trip channels; send to GRPS curriculum coordinators + 5 target corporate contacts
- [ ] Attraction Pass GR application submitted (through EXGR)

### Key Milestone
**Founding Membership goal: 200 households enrolled (pre-opening)**
Revenue: 200 × $120 avg = **$24,000 pre-revenue from memberships alone**

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Pop-up event costs (tent, signage, samples) | $800–1,500 |
| Paid social ad campaigns | $1,500–3,000/month |
| Group sales deck design | $1,000–2,000 (one-time) |
| **Month 3 Total** | **$3,300–6,500** |

## Month 4 (T–3): Pre-Sale Launch

**Goal:** Pre-sell 500+ guided tour tickets; fill first 4 weeks of programming; generate $35K+ in pre-revenue

### Deliverables
- [ ] Online booking system live (FareHarbor, Xola, or Checkfront — industry-standard agri-tourism platforms; ~2.6% transaction fee)
- [ ] First wave of pre-sale tour dates released: 6 weeks of Saturday tours (2/day × 6 weeks = 72 tour sessions × avg 15 guests = 1,080 potential tickets)
- [ ] Workshop pre-sales: first 6 weeks of beekeeping intro (12 sessions × 15 seats = 180 pre-sale seats)
- [ ] Hotel concierge kits delivered to all 6 target downtown hotels: printed brochure + QR code + 2 complimentary tour tickets per property
- [ ] WZZM 13 and FOX 17 morning show pitch: "Meet the people growing food on the Grand River" — request feature segment 6–8 weeks pre-open
- [ ] First farm-to-table dinner pre-sale (8 weeks out): 50 seats at $125 = $6,250 from one event

### Pre-Revenue Target Breakdown
| Source | Revenue |
|--------|---------|
| Founding memberships (350 HH × $120) | $42,000 |
| Pre-sold tour tickets (500 × $26) | $13,000 |
| Workshop pre-sales (180 seats × $95 avg) | $17,100 |
| Pre-sold dinner (50 seats × $125) | $6,250 |
| **Total pre-opening revenue** | **$78,350** |

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Booking platform setup | $500–1,500 |
| Hotel concierge kits (6 hotels × $150) | $900 |
| Media pitch / PR support | $1,000–2,500 |
| Paid social (pre-sale conversion campaign) | $2,000–4,000 |
| **Month 4 Total** | **$4,400–8,900** |

## Month 5 (T–8 to T–5 weeks): Final Push

**Goal:** Sell out opening weekend; secure 5+ earned media placements; FAM tours complete

### Deliverables
- [ ] **Hotel Concierge FAM Tour** (T–8 weeks): Guided 45-minute experiential tour for concierge staff at all downtown hotels; tasting included; leave booking materials
- [ ] **EXGR Group Sales FAM Tour** (T–6 weeks): Tour for EXGR sales managers and West Michigan Sports Commission contacts; demo the corporate retreat and group dining formats
- [ ] **Media FAM Day** (T–5 weeks): All local media invited — MLive, WZZM, FOX 17, GR Magazine, Rapid Growth; guided tour + photo op + hosted tasting; embargo lift = opening week
- [ ] Email: "Opening Weekend is Sold Out [almost]" scarcity campaign
- [ ] Google My Business listing active + verified
- [ ] TripAdvisor listing created; first 10 reviews seeded via friends/family/founding members
- [ ] Partnership announcement with EXGR and Downtown Market GR via coordinated social posts

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| FAM tour events (food, materials, 3 events) | $2,500–4,500 |
| Final advertising push (digital) | $2,000–4,000 |
| Influencer/creator preview event (food + experience for 15 creators) | $1,500–2,500 |
| **Month 5 Total** | **$6,000–11,000** |

## Month 6 / Launch Week (T–2 weeks to T+0)

**Goal:** Maximum earned media; sellout opening week; community galvanized

### Deliverables
- [ ] **Grand Opening Event** (Thursday evening, invite only): Press + partners + founding members + local civic leaders; 100–150 guests; tasting + apiary demo + ribbon cutting with DGRI and EXGR representatives
- [ ] **Public Opening Weekend**: Full tour schedule; retail open; honey and harvest products available
- [ ] Social media: daily posts; Stories documenting opening day; encourage guests to tag + share
- [ ] **Influencer/creator content goes live**: seeded in previous month's preview; organic reach surge
- [ ] WZZM and FOX 17 segments air (coordinated with embargo lift)
- [ ] Email to full list: "The doors are open. Come grow with us."

### Budget Band
| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Grand Opening event (catering, décor, A/V) | $4,000–8,000 |
| Opening week social ad boost | $1,000–2,000 |
| Grand Opening PR coordination | $1,500–3,000 |
| **Launch Week Total** | **$6,500–13,000** |

---

## Post-Open: Months 1–3 (Retain + Grow)

**Goal:** Drive reviews, referrals, and repeat visitation; grow membership from 350 to 600 HH

### Key Tactics
- **Review activation:** Automated post-visit email requesting TripAdvisor + Google review (target: 100 reviews in first 60 days)
- **"Bring a Friend" promotion:** Founding Members receive 1 guest ticket credit to share in months 1–3
- **Monthly email newsletter:** "What's Growing This Week" — seasonal updates, upcoming events, workshop announcements
- **GRPS school group season:** Outreach to all Kent County schools in September (Year 2 if Hub opens spring; Year 1 if opens fall)
- **Corporate pipeline follow-up:** All FAM tour contacts receive personal follow-up email + phone call 2 weeks post-open
