Dear Experience Grand Rapids Team,

I'm writing to introduce the **Grand Rapids CEA Agritech Hub** — a first-of-its-kind hydro-powered controlled environment agriculture campus opening in downtown Grand Rapids, situated along the Grand River corridor.

We believe what you've built in Grand Rapids tourism is extraordinary. [Record numbers in 2025 — 2.36 million hotel rooms sold and $290.6M in hotel revenue](https://www.experiencegr.com/media-articles/post/grand-rapids-and-kent-county-tourism-sales-reach-record-levels-in-2025/) confirm what we see on every block downtown: Grand Rapids has become a true destination. We want to be part of what makes it even more compelling.

**What We're Building**

The Hub is a fully indoor, climate-controlled CEA facility that grows food year-round using hydro-electric power from the Grand River. It houses:
- Hydroponic growing towers (leafy greens, herbs, microgreens, edibles)
- An on-site apiary with 100,000+ bees (observation + experience programming)
- A food processing and honey extraction center
- A fully accessible event and dining space with riverfront orientation

For visitors, we offer guided greenhouse tours, adult workshops (beekeeping, hydroponics, fermentation), farm-to-table dinners, corporate retreats, school field trips, and an annual membership program. We expect to host **40,000–60,000 paid guest experiences annually** at an average yield of $25–30/person.

**Why This Partnership Matters**

The Lower Grand River restoration — with in-river construction beginning in 2025 — is creating a new active-leisure corridor that will bring a fresh wave of outdoor and adventure-oriented visitors to the waterfront. The Hub is positioned to capture those visitors and convert a walk along the river into a full 2-hour immersive food and nature experience.

For your group sales and convention business, the Hub offers something rare: a memorable off-site experience that is **fully weatherproof, ADA accessible, and genuinely unique to Grand Rapids.** It's an ideal addition to convention delegate itineraries, pre/post-conference programming, and curated FAM tour agendas.

1. Be considered for **Official Attraction Partner** status and inclusion in the Experience Grand Rapids Visitor Guide and digital itinerary builder

3. Explore inclusion in the **Attraction Pass GR** program if applicable
4. Discuss co-marketing opportunities aligned with the river corridor narrative

We're currently in the final months before our public opening and are building our partner ecosystem now. We'd love to have Experience Grand Rapids at the table from the beginning.

Would you have 20–30 minutes for a call in the next few weeks? I'm happy to work around your schedule or come to your office for an in-person introduction.
